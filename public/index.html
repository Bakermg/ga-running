Welcome to North Georgia Running

North Georgia has miles and miles of awesome twisty, challenging, running trails and roads for running. There are numerous running events happening all around North Georgia every weekend, be it an ultra-marathon, marathon, half-marathon or 5k/10k race. We have it all here in North Georgia and this is the place to come to find information for all things running here.
